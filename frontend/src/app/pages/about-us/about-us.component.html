About TeKSED Inc.

TeKSED Inc. aims to be Africa’s leading technology company, an innovative force that uses advanced solutions to reshape industries, empower communities, and set new global standards. Anchored in African heritage and guided by future-focused strategies, TeKSED Inc. seeks to establish a new benchmark in sustainable and inclusive technology.

Our Story

We are TeKSED Inc driven by one belief: Africa doesn’t just consume technology, she creates.

Since our founding in late 2024, we’ve set out on a bold journey to build the continent’s first world-class tech giant, one that solves real challenges, empowers communities, and inspires the next generation of innovators.

2020

Founded in Kumasi with 5 young leaders with a shared passion to transform Africa through technology.

Our Purpose

Driving innovation with purpose and passion

Vision Statement

To become the leading global technology giant, rooted in Africa, revolutionizing industries and empowering the world with innovative, sustainable, and inclusive solutions.

Mission Statement

To pioneer groundbreaking technologies that transform lives, foster economic growth, and position Africa as a global leader in the tech landscape, while delivering excellence and value to our customers, partners, and communities while uplifting society through intentional charity and mission work.
